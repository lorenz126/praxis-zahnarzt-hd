# Ritually Health

A modern preventive health practice website focused on wellness, longevity, and proactive care.

## Vision

Instead of treating what's broken, we prevent things from breaking in the first place. Ritually is a new kind of medical practice combining cutting-edge diagnostics with personalized treatment plans.

## Target Audience

- Young professionals seeking proactive health optimization
- Young mothers looking to restore vitality and wellness

## Services

### Diagnostics
- **Health, Nutrition & Lifestyle Consulting** - Comprehensive assessment of your current state
- **Aesthetic Diagnostics** - Professional evaluation of skin health and aging factors
- **Cosmetic Diagnostics** - Personalized analysis for cosmetic treatment planning

### Treatments
- **Nutrition Plans** - Tailored dietary programs based on your diagnostics
- **Aesthetic Treatments** - Evidence-based procedures for skin health and rejuvenation
- **Cosmetic Treatments** - Advanced cosmetic procedures
- **Wellness Infusions** - IV therapy and vitamin infusions for optimal wellness

### Routines
- Personalized maintenance programs
- Long-term wellness protocols

## Tech Stack

- **Framework**: React 18 + Vite
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Future**: Booking system integration, mobile app wrapper

## Design

**Visual Style**: Minimalist & Clean
**Colors**: Sage Green primary (#87A878), forest accents (#2D4739), warm neutrals (#FAFAF8)
**Typography**: Elegant serif headings (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif body (Inter)
